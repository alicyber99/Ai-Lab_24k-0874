1. Probability of drawing a red card
A standard deck has 52 cards
Red cards = Hearts (13) + Diamonds (13) = 26
P(Red) = 26/52 = 1/2

2. Given red card probability it is a heart
P(Heart | Red ) = 13 / 26 = 1/2

3. Given face card probability it is a diamond
Face cards = Jack, Queen, King
Each suit has 3 
total = 12 face cards
Diamonds face cards = 3
P(Diamond|Face) = 3/12 = 1/4

4. Given face card → probability it is spade OR queen
Face cards = 12
Spade face cards = 3
Queens (all suits) = 4
Overlap (Queen of Spades) = 1
P( Spade OR Queen) = (3+4-1)/12 = 6/12 = 1/2
