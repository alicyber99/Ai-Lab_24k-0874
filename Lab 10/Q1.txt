Data Cleaning & Encoding
Missing values:
Numerical  fill with mean/median
Categorical (neighborhood) → fill with mode
Encode neighborhood using:
One-Hot Encoding (preferred)
● Relevant Features
Most impactful features:
Square footage (strongest impact)
Number of bedrooms & bathrooms
Location (neighborhood)
Age of house
● Model & Evaluation
Model used: Linear Regression
Evaluation metrics:
MAE (Mean Absolute Error)
MSE (Mean Squared Error)
R² Score
Good model → low MAE/MSE, high R² (close to 1)
